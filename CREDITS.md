## Ephemerides

Planet, Sun, Moon, and Galilean-moon positions are computed with
[astronomy-engine](https://github.com/cosinekitty/astronomy) by Don Cross (MIT).
Other moons use mean osculating Keplerian elements compiled from NASA/JPL fact
sheets.
