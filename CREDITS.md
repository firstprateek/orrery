Notes: the Titan map is the near-infrared surface mosaic (grayscale, tinted in
the app); some maps have unimaged dark regions (Pluto's far south, Triton's far
north, Charon-era gaps) — those black zones are honest, not bugs.

## Star catalog

Bright-star positions, magnitudes, and temperatures from the **Yale Bright Star
Catalog** (public domain compilation), baked to a binary at build time.

## Ephemerides

Planet, Sun, Moon, and Galilean-moon positions are computed with
[astronomy-engine](https://github.com/cosinekitty/astronomy) by Don Cross (MIT).
Other moons use mean osculating Keplerian elements compiled from NASA/JPL fact
sheets.
